## Deferred Candidate

### Non-recursive gate-regression verification

- Axiom Forge self-changes are currently verified during promotion by the configured unit command, not by the full runner and promotion matrices.
- Directly requiring `scripts/forge_check.sh` during promotion is unsafe or recursive: `verify_patch.sh` applies candidate patches in an uncommitted disposable worktree while `forge_check.sh` requires a clean worktree, and `forge_check.sh` runs promotion tests that invoke `promote.sh` and re-enter verification.
- No narrow implementation slice is approved for v0.2 at this time.
- Future work requires a separately designed non-recursive gate-contract test architecture.
- Existing `forge_check.sh` remains the full manual health proof.
